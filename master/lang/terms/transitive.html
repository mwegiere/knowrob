 transitive(+Query) is nondet
Assumes transitivity of a 2-ary or 3-ary query term, meaning that if query holds for (A,B) and (B,C), then it also holds for (A,C). In the case of a 2-ary query term, the two arguments are assumed to be the subject and the object of the relation. In case of 3-ary queries, the object is the last argument, and the middle argument is a property.

Note that transitivity of subClassOf and subPropertyOf is already handled on the DB level such that no sequential querying is needed for these cases.

Arguments:
Query- A query term.
